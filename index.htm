I am currently a first-year Phd student at Computer Science & Engineering Department, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Jiaya Jia.

Prior to that, I graduated from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in July 2019. I have been lucky to work with Prof. Fumin Shen(UESTC) and Chen Qian (SenseTime) during my internship at SenseTime Research, as well as Wayne Wu and Dr. Xiaoyong Shen when I was a research intern at Youtu X-Lab at Tencent.

My research interests lie primarily in computer vision and deep learning, particularly focusing on human face/body understanding and representation learning, such as video supervised learning.
